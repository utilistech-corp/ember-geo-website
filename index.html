FAQ

Common questions about GEO

What is Generative Engine Optimisation (GEO)?

GEO is the practice of optimising your website and content so that AI-powered search engines — like ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Perplexity, and Claude — cite your business in their answers. It's the AI-era equivalent of SEO, focused on earning citations rather than ranking links.

How is GEO different from SEO?

SEO focuses on ranking your website in traditional search results (ten blue links). GEO focuses on getting your brand mentioned and cited by AI engines that generate direct answers. You still need SEO as a foundation — GEO builds on top of it to capture the fast-growing AI search channel.

Do I still need SEO if I invest in GEO?

Yes. AI engines heavily reference traditional search results when building their answers. Strong SEO makes GEO more effective — they work together. We recommend a combined approach, and our audits cover both.

How long before I see results?

Most clients see their first AI citations within 4–8 weeks of implementing technical and content changes. Significant, measurable growth in AI referral traffic typically happens within 3 months. The timeline depends on your current content quality and competitive landscape.

Which AI platforms do you optimise for?

We optimise for all major generative search engines: ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews (and AI Mode), Perplexity, Claude, Gemini, Microsoft Copilot, and emerging platforms. Our approach is engine-agnostic — the fundamentals of citability work across all AI models.

What does the free GEO audit include?

Our free audit scores your website 0–100 across six categories: AI citability, brand authority, content quality, technical foundations, structured data, and platform optimisation. You'll receive a report with prioritised recommendations and a clear roadmap for improvement.
